    Unique intense 1664 Rosé.

    1664 Rosé is original with a very complex fruitiness characterized by raspberry flavours with subtle notes of exotic fruit and coriander. The pink-amber colour and hazy body with white head make 1664 Rosé a light refreshing wheat beer treat with a fruity twist.

    Ingredients:

    Water, barley malt, wheat, sugar, glucose syrup, flavouring, elderberry juice from concentrate (0,5%), raspberry juice from concentrate (0,1%), acidity regulator: citric acid, hops, orange peel, coriander
